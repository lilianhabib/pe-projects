About Us

That Food Truck was established at the start of 2022 by Ren and Naya, who are passionate about providing quality service and cuisine. Ren and Naya have had years of experience serving, tasting, and creating Indian cuisine. That Food Truck specialized Indian delicacies with a twist of modern flavours. Their unique dishes will truly be a treat to your eyes and taste buds.
